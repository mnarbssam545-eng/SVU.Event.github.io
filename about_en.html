About SVU Events

The SVU Events platform is a digital directory dedicated to showcasing the events of the Syrian Virtual University, aiming to facilitate students' access to cultural, scientific, and recreational activities.

Our vision is to build an active and connected university community by publishing events in an organized and accessible manner.

Our mission is to support communication among students and encourage participation in various university activities.

Event Publishing Policies

  • ✔️ The event must be related to the university or the student community.
  • ✔️ Publishing inappropriate or non-academic content is strictly prohibited.
  • ✔️ Clear information must be provided: Date, Location, and Description.
  • ✔️ Events are reviewed before being published on the platform.
